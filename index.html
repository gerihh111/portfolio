CocoPearls (Ender pearl protection plugin)

A must-have for PvP servers to stop all ender pearl glitches and fine-tune pearl behavior.

Features

  • Blocks ender pearl glitches
  • Teammate pearl passthrough (team hooks)
  • Custom cooldown, damage, and endermite chance
  • Refund on failed landings
  • Particle customization and throw strength control
  • Supports tight spaces with swimming-mode pearls

Technical

  • Easy to configure
  • Paper/Folia compatible
  • Lightweight
  • Team plugin hooks available

CocoLevels (Leveling and conversion plugin)

Level system with multi-database support, GUI changers, placeholders, and converters between XP, levels, currency, and commands.

Features

  • Levels with XP tracking and toplists
  • GUI changers for XP, levels, currency, commands
  • PlaceholderAPI and HeadDatabase hooks
  • Level converter and configurable formats

Technical

  • SQLite, MySQL, PostgreSQL, MariaDB, MongoDB
  • Configurable table prefix and drivers
  • Custom placeholders for XP, level, toplists
  • GUI-defined actions and currencies
